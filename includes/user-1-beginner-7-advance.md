## Keyboard Shortcuts

`ALT-o`: organization <br>
`ALT-d`: entity in design <br>
`ALT-t`: entity in transition <br>
`ALT-p`: entity in operate <br>
`ALT-g`: any entity and narrow with `d`, `t` or `o` <br>
